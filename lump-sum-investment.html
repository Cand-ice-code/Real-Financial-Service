Lump Sum Investment

A Lump Sum Investment allows you to invest a once-off amount of money into a professionally managed portfolio, with the goal of growing your wealth over time. Whether you're investing R20,000 or R2 million, this is a flexible and effective way to put your money to work.

Unlike monthly contributions, a lump sum gives your investment a head start, allowing compounding returns to start working immediately.

Why Choose a Lump Sum Investment?

  • One-Time Contribution: Invest once—no monthly debit orders or commitments required.
  • Flexible Access: Choose between short-, medium-, or long-term investment horizons depending on your goals.
  • Tailored Portfolios: Your money is invested according to your risk appetite—whether conservative, balanced, or aggressive.
  • Diversified Growth: Funds are spread across shares, bonds, property, and cash for optimal risk-adjusted returns.

Perfect for Goals Like:

  • Investing an inheritance or work bonus
  • Saving for a child’s education or wedding
  • Planning a future deposit on a home
  • Building long-term wealth for retirement or financial freedom
