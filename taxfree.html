What is a Tax-Free Savings Investment?

A Tax-Free Savings Account (TFSA) is a smart, simple way to grow your money without paying tax on the interest, dividends, or capital gains. It’s ideal for medium- to long-term goals like education, a first home deposit, or simply building wealth—100% tax-free.

Why Choose a Tax-Free Savings Investment?

  • No Tax on Growth: All the returns you earn—whether from interest, dividends, or capital growth—are completely tax-free.
  • Flexible Access: You can withdraw funds at any time, without penalties, making this a versatile investment tool.
  • Simple and Transparent: No hidden fees, no complicated structures—just straightforward saving and compounding growth.
  • Government-Approved: Designed to encourage South Africans to save, with annual and lifetime contribution limits.

How Much Can You Invest?

  • R36,000 per year (that’s just R3,000 per month)
  • R500,000 lifetime maximum

Going over these limits means penalties—so we help you manage your contributions to stay compliant and fully benefit from tax-free growth.

Who Should Consider a TFSA?

  • First-time investors looking to start saving smart
  • Parents investing for their children’s education
  • Individuals planning for future expenses or financial freedom
  • Anyone who wants long-term growth without SARS taking a share
