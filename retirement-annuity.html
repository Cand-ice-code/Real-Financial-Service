Retirement Annuity

What is a Retirement Annuity?

A Retirement Annuity (RA) is a powerful, tax-efficient way to save for your retirement. It is designed to help you build long-term wealth, even if your employer doesn’t offer a pension or provident fund.

With a Retirement Annuity, you make regular contributions into a professionally managed investment portfolio. Over time, these contributions grow with the market, helping you accumulate the funds you’ll need to enjoy a financially secure retirement.

Why Choose a Retirement Annuity?

  • Tax Benefits: You can deduct up to 27.5% of your taxable income (up to R350,000 per year) in RA contributions, reducing the amount of tax you pay annually.
  • Disciplined Saving: Funds in a retirement annuity are preserved until you turn 55, which helps prevent early withdrawals and ensures long-term growth.
  • Flexible Contributions: You decide how much you want to contribute monthly or annually—and can increase or pause contributions if your financial situation changes.
  • Access to Expert Fund Managers: Your money is invested in a diversified portfolio, professionally managed to balance growth and risk.

Who Should Consider a Retirement Annuity?

  • Self-employed professionals or freelancers with no employer retirement fund.
  • Employees who want to supplement their existing pension or provident fund.
  • Anyone looking for tax relief while building long-term retirement wealth.
