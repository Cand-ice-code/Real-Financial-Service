Frequently Asked Questions

Funeral cover provides financial assistance to cover funeral costs when a loved one passes away. It helps ease the financial burden during a difficult time.

Life cover pays a lump sum to your beneficiaries if you pass away, helping them maintain financial stability.

A retirement annuity is a long-term investment plan that helps you save for retirement with tax benefits and professional fund management.

Claims can be made by contacting our customer service team via phone or email. We aim to process claims quickly and efficiently.

We offer low-cost plans designed to suit various budgets without compromising on cover quality.
